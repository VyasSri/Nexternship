About Us

What is Nexternship?

We are a platform that was founded by high schoolers who struggled to find internships and work experience. We created this platform to assist students of the future in gaining real-world experience and thriving in corporate environments, while excelling technically!

What Do We Do?

By gathering the information of students along with their interests, we connect them to companies with open positions. This provides high schoolers with a feel for a work environment and gives them valuable experience they can use in future jobs and internships.

Our Mission

Our goal is to establish a long-standing connection between high schoolers and local businesses, ensuring mutual success and prosperity for all involved.
