About Us

What is Nexternship?

We are a platform founded by high schoolers who had trouble finding technical experience to use in professional fields. We wanted to give the students of tomorrow the opportunity to excel with qualities that real-world companies value.

What Do We Do?

By identifying the experiences, interests, and profiles of students, we can connect them to relevant workshops in the fields they desired, posted by reputable and respected companies.

Our Mission

Our goal is to establish a long-standing connection between high schoolers and local businesses, ensuring mutual success and prosperity for all involved. As a byproduct of this, we will be molding the students of tomorrow into a competent batch of leaders.
