About Us

What is NExternship?

We are an NGO that was founded by high schoolers who struggled to find internships and work experience. We decided found this company to assist students of the future

What Do We Do?

By gathering the information of students as well as their interests, we can connect them to companies who have open positions which give high schoolers a feel for a
work environment and give them valuable experience that they could use in future jobs, internships, etc. 

Our Mission

Our goal is to establish a long standing connection between high schoolers of the locality and businesses, ensuring mutual success and prosperity for all parties involved.
